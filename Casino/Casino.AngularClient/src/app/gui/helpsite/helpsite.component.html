Rules for Online Casino:

  • Players must be of legal gambling age in their jurisdiction to participate.
  • Users must create an account with accurate information to play.
  • Players deposit funds into their accounts using approved payment methods. Withdrawals are subject to verification and may have processing times.
  • Games use certified Random Number Generators (RNGs) for fairness.
  • Casinos use encryption and secure transactions to protect player data.
  • Casinos operate under licenses from recognized gambling authorities.

Rules for Online Roulette:

  • Players place bets on a virtual roulette table before the spin.
  • Bets can be placed on individual numbers, groups of numbers, colors, odd/even, etc.
  • The virtual roulette wheel is spun, and a ball is dropped.
  • The winning number/color is determined when the ball comes to rest.
  • Payouts are made according to the type of bet placed and the game's payout rules.

Rules for Online Slot Machines (Slots):

  • Slots have reels that spin with various symbols.
  • Players can bet on multiple paylines, and payouts depend on symbol combinations aligning on active paylines.
  • Special symbols like Wilds and Scatters offer additional features or bonuses.
  • Some slots have bonus rounds triggered by specific symbol combinations or actions.
  • Players can adjust bet size and spin the reels manually or use auto-spin features.
  • Slot outcomes are determined by RNGs to ensure fair gameplay.
